FEATURES




Customized Navigation

Find a place you want to explore and pick a navigator to start your Jumper journey!

Experience scenes from far away instantly by one-to-one live streaming!

Fun Interaction

Use gestures and stickers to have a fun interaction with the navigator!

Jumper Energy

Demo Video




Jumper Teaser

Jumper app helps users display scenes around the world to people who haven't been there. It provides live streaming and interactions to make users have real time expierence.

Ideation




  • Original Intention

    We want to provide a fun and instant platform for users.

  • Design Concept

    Explorers can see scenes from far away to fulfill their curiosity. On the other hand, navigators can feel accomplished by sharing their experience.

  • Core Value

    We provide Jumper an intuitive way to explore the world, expand horizon, and moreover actually travel.
